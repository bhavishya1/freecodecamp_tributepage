Brief Biography

Billy Joel was born May 9, 1949 in The Bronx, New York. He begun playing piano at a piano bar to help his mother.

His first solo album was titled Cold Spring Harbor in which popular songs "She's Got a Way" and "Everybody Loves You Now" were originally written. Joel moved to columbia records and released Piano Man in 1973. "Piano Man" quickly became his signature song and he started ending all of his concerts with it. Joel went on to write many more great songs and albums.

Billy Joel has been married multiple time throughout his life. He currently is married to his fourth wife Alexis Roderick. The ceremony was on July 4, 2015. Joel battled depression for many years and attempted to commit suicide by drinking furniture polish.


To read more about Billy Joel, check out his wikipedia page at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billy_Joel
